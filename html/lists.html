How to prepare Chapati
make handsize pieces of the dough. Dust a rolling board or flat surface with a little flour.Take one dough ball, flatten it slightly, and roll it out into a thin, round disc (about 6-8 inches in diameter). Dust with more flour as needed to prevent sticking.
Cook the Chapatis
Heat a tava, griddle, or non-stick pan over medium-high heat. Place the rolled-out chapati on the hot pan. Cook until bubbles start to form on the surface (about 30 seconds), then flip it over. Cook the other side for about 30 seconds. Flip again, pressing gently with a cloth or spatula to help it puff up. Cook until you see light brown spots on both sides.
